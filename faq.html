Loan Repayment

Weekly means you repay the loan at weekly intervals. Weekly loan term has three options:

  • Weekly 3 (Your repayment is equally divided into 3 weeks)
  • Weekly 4 (Your repayment is equally divided into 4 weeks)
  • Weekly 5 (Your repayment is equally divided into 5 weeks)

Example: Let’s say you got your loan on June 6 and you have chosen the Weekly 3, then your repayment schedule will be:

  • 1st repayment: June 13
  • 2nd repayment: June 20
  • 3rd repayment: June 27

If you got your loan on June 6 and you have chosen the Weekly 4:

  • 1st repayment: June 13
  • 2nd repayment: June 20
  • 3rd repayment: June 27
  • 4th repayment: July 4

If you got your loan on June 6 and you have chosen the Weekly 5:

  • 1st repayment: June 13
  • 2nd repayment: June 20
  • 3rd repayment: June 27
  • 4th repayment: July 4
  • 5th repayment: July 11

Bi-Weekly means you repay the loan in every 2 weeks. Bi-Weekly loan term has three options:

  • Bi-Weekly 1 ( Settle your loan after 2 weeks)
  • Bi-Weekly 2 (Your repayment is equally divided into 2)
  • Bi-Weekly 3 (Your repayment is equally divided into 3)

Let’s say you got your loan on June 6 and you have chosen the Bi-Weekly 1, then your repayment schedule will be:

  • 1st repayment: June 20

If you got your loan on June 6 and you have chosen the Bi-Weekly 2:

  • 1st repayment: June 20
  • 2nd repayment: July 4

If you got your loan on June 6 and you have chosen the Bi-Weekly 3:

  • 1st repayment: June 20
  • 2nd repayment: July 4
  • 3rd repayment: July 18

Monthly loan term means you have to settle the loan after 28 days. Let’s say you got your loan on November 10 and you have chosen the Monthly Term. Then you have to repay the loan with its interest rate on December 4.

If your repayment due date falls on a non-working holiday, you have to make your repayment on the last working day before the holiday. Let’s say your due date is on December 30, 2016, Rizal Day. Then you have to pay on December 29.

Your repayment schedule will be discussed with our staff via phone call and it will also be sent to your email.
